About Me.

I am Gunesh Ravi, a dedicated Chef de Partie with over 14 years of professional experience in international kitchens. My expertise spans Italian, Sushi, and Indian cuisines, with a strong focus on flavor, precision, and presentation.

Throughout my career, I have worked in fast-paced, high-pressure environments, mastering station management, team collaboration, and training junior staff. I take pride in maintaining the highest standards of food quality while ensuring efficiency and consistency across every dish.

Beyond my technical skills, I bring creativity, adaptability, and leadership, always striving to inspire my team and deliver exceptional dining experiences. I believe that cooking is not just about food — it’s about creating lasting memories through taste and presentation.
